About Us

Welcome to our professional air conditioner maintenance company. Mian Gee Air Conditioner Maintenance is a professional air conditioning repair company based in Abu Dhabi. We are a team of highly skilled and experienced technicians who are dedicated to providing the best repair services for various brands of air conditioners, washing machines, and fridges. At MIAN GEE AIR CONDITIONER company, we understand how crucial it is to have a fully functional air conditioning system, especially during the hot summer months in Abu Dhabi. That’s why we offer comprehensive repair services for different types of air conditioning units, including duct units, FC units, and split units. Our team is equipped with the latest tools and equipment to diagnose any issues and resolve them in a timely manner. We believe that customer satisfaction is paramount, which is why we offer door-to-door services to ensure that our clients’ needs are met conveniently. Our technicians will come to your location and provide on-site repairs, saving you time and hassle. Our team is available around the clock, providing on-call services 24/7. So whether it’s a minor repair or a major overhaul, you can contact us at any time and our team will be ready to serve you. As a leading company in the industry, we take pride in our commitment to excellence, professionalism, and customer satisfaction. Our services are designed to exceed your expectations and keep your appliances running smoothly for years to come.
